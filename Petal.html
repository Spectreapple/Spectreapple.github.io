This is a collaboration project called Petal,

The games foundation is a 2D Adventure platformer

Petal is made in unity and uses the language c#
The main protagonist named Atra is based of the mushroom (Coprinopsis atramentaria)
also known as the Inkycap
This type of mushroom looks scary and poisonous
This makes Atra look violent and scary which she is quite the opposite
The game is currently in Aplha development and is being worked on.
